# 静室 · 单一虚拟形象 — 图像生成提示词

> 用于给 静室 的唯一 AI 陪伴者做一个虚拟形象。每个选项是一段可直接贴进图像生成工具（Midjourney / DALL·E / Stitch / Claude 图像）的英文提示词（英文出图更稳）。
> 气质统一:低饱和鼠尾草绿 + 雾蓝 + 暖奶白,温暖、平静、被接住。方形、居中、可当头像、小尺寸也清楚、画面内不要文字。
>
> ⚠️ 诚实红线:别做成写实真人脸 / 白大褂 / 医生形象——会让人误以为是"持证真人治疗师"。**推荐选 1 或 3(非人/抽象),最稳妥也最贴气质。**

---

## 选项 1 ·【推荐】抽象温暖的"存在感"(非拟人)
```
A soft, abstract glowing presence representing a calm AI companion — a gently
luminous orb of warm sage-green and misty blue light with softly diffused edges,
like breath fogging glass, a faint concentric halo around it. Minimalist, serene,
weightless. Warm cream background (#FAF9F7) with subtle aged-paper texture. No face,
no body — a comforting presence, not a person. Soft ambient light, low contrast, no
neon. Refined editorial flat-illustration style. Square, centered, avatar-ready.
```

## 选项 2 · 象征"静室"的意象(一间被点亮的安静的房间)
```
A minimalist symbolic emblem for "静室 / Quiet Room": a single softly-lit paper
window or warm paper lantern glowing in a calm dim space, sage-green and warm amber
light, lots of negative space, tranquil and literary. Hand-drawn editorial line work
with a soft watercolor wash. Warm, safe, contemplative, no people. Square logo/avatar
composition, centered, works at small sizes.
```

## 选项 3 ·【推荐】温柔的非人陪伴体
```
A gentle, simple, rounded companion character for a mental-health app — a soft
abstract figure made of warm sage-green and cream tones, calm half-closed eyes,
serene expression, a tiny soft smile, plush minimal form. Warmth like Finch or
Headspace but more grown-up and literary, never childish or cartoonish. Soft matte
shading, no harsh outlines, low contrast. Warm cream background. Square, centered,
approachable yet calm. No text.
```

## 选项 4 · 克制的、明显插画风的人形(若一定要拟人)
```
A serene, androgynous, clearly illustrated (NOT photorealistic) head-and-shoulders
portrait representing a warm listener — soft features, gentle calm gaze or closed
eyes, no medical cues, no white coat, no stethoscope. Muted sage-green
and warm cream palette, soft painterly editorial style, gentle diffused light. Calm,
safe, warm. Deliberately stylized so it reads as a symbol of warmth, not a real
clinician. Square avatar, centered, soft background.
```

---

## 通用要求(贴在任意一条后面)
```
Style: calm, warm, low-saturation, editorial, premium, lots of negative space.
Palette: sage green, misty blue, warm cream; one soft coral only if an accent is
needed. Avoid: neon, harsh contrast, pure black/white, hospital blue,
stock-photo realism, busy detail. Output: 1:1 square, centered, clean margins, no
text in the image. Provide a version that also reads well on a dark (#0E0E0E) UI.
```

> 出 2–3 版挑一个;选定后如果想要"说话时的轻微呼吸/发光"动效,可以再让设计基于静态图加一个很克制的 idle 动画(别花)。
